Healthcare cyber attacks are on the rise

The number of cyber attacks is on the rise and healthcare organizations are the prime target because of its unique vulnerabilities. As data becomes the new health care currency, protecting it will be key. Rapid transformations in clinical innovations, connected medical devices, and market complexity have amplified the need for its protection.

The aftermath of a cyber attack can have devastating impacts to patients and healthcare organizations including regulatory fines, reputational damage, lawsuites, and patient mortality.
